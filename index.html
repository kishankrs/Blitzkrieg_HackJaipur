Unified Covid Management Dashboard







About UCMD

This dashboard is a prototype for COVID management that will help district or block level officers in keeping track of hospital performance. The performance can be tracked on multiple parameters such as the number of beds, ICUs, cases reported to a particular hospital.


Actionable Insights

UCMD will help the officers understand how a particular hospital is faring when it comes to the rate of recovery from a particular area or medical facilities. It will make decision making easier for the officers on managing requests by the medical fraternity.


Mission

We love finding patterns in data and any anomaly must be presented to the decision-maker immediately. It will not only help save precious lives but also ensure a stronger healthcare ecosystem.


Futurescope

We aim to build a robust data analysis system on top of the management dashboard. Our team has UI/UX, AI/ML and Full stack development enthusiasts.
